A B O U T

Deluna Dessert Cafe opened its doors in December of 2019.
As a Korean-American, Jeff wanted to bring the street foods he knew and loved to Sacramento, CA.
With beginnings in boba drinks, Deluna has introduced Bingsoo, Taiyaki, Tteokbooki, and Korean-style Corn Dogs to the ever-growing menu.
Stop by our location to try the classics and discover new favorites!
